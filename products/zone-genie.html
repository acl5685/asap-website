How it works

Everything a planner checks, done automatically.

Zone Genie reads the application, pulls the parcel, references your zoning code, and returns a complete review in under 70 seconds. The output is structured for staff — not a raw code dump.

01

Built for the queue

Fits the workflow your department already has. Applicants submit through your portal. Genie runs the review. Staff review structured output — not a raw code dump.

02

Validated, not vibes

Every finding is traceable to a specific code section. Genie cites its source. Staff can verify in seconds — and residents get answers that hold up at appeal.

03

Trustworthy by design

Genie flags what it cannot determine. No hallucinated approvals. When something requires human judgment — variance requests, edge cases — it says so clearly.
