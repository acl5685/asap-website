The site-selection desk you'd hire — for every parcel, on demand.

Stop spending Tuesdays pulling parcel data and Thursdays cross-referencing FEMA panels. Site Genie does it once, fast, with the sources cited — so your team can spend its time on the deals that need judgment.

Why it matters

Site selection is the slowest, most error-prone part of development.

The deals you win are the ones you find first. The deals you lose money on are the ones whose boundaries you didn't read carefully enough. Site Genie compresses both into a single workflow.

The problem

  • Site selection requires hours of manual research
  • Zoning compatibility varies by parcel and is complex
  • Developers miss opportunities due to slow analysis
  • Cross-referencing spatial data is error-prone
  • No single tool brings it all together

With Site Genie

  • AI-powered site scoring in seconds
  • Instant zoning compatibility checks per parcel
  • Spatial overlays reveal hidden opportunities
  • Development feasibility assessed automatically
  • Integrated with the full ASAP platform
