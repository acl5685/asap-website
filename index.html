How It Works

Everything a planner checks, done automatically.

01

Built for the Queue

Zone Genie fits the workflow your department already has. Applicants submit. Genie runs the review. Staff review the structured output.

02

Validated, Not Vibes

Every finding is traceable to a specific code section. Genie cites its source. Staff can verify in seconds — and residents get answers.

03

Trustworthy by Design

Genie flags what it cannot determine. When something requires human judgment — variance requests, edge cases — it says so clearly.

The Time Math

What happens to the hours your staff used to spend.

A standard zoning review — pulling the parcel, reading the district code, checking setbacks, flagging use compliance — takes a skilled planner 1 to 1.5 hours per application. Zone Genie does the same work in under 70 seconds.

That time doesn't disappear. It moves to the work that still requires a human: site visits, community input, complex projects. The queue moves. Staff morale improves.
